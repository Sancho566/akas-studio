Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our design process and services.

Our design process begins with an initial consultation to understand your vision, needs, and budget. We then conduct a site analysis, develop conceptual designs, refine the selected concept, and oversee the implementation. The process typically takes 8-12 weeks depending on project scope.

Our fees vary based on project scope, size, and complexity. We offer both flat-fee and hourly rate options. Residential projects typically range from $5,000-$50,000+, while commercial projects start at $10,000. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a detailed estimate tailored to your specific needs.

Yes, we have an extensive network of trusted contractors, artisans, and vendors that we've worked with for years. We can recommend professionals for your project or work with your preferred contractors. We also provide project management services to ensure quality execution of our designs.

While our studio is based in New York, we serve clients nationwide and internationally. We've completed projects in major cities across the U.S. including Los Angeles, Miami, Chicago, and Boston. For remote projects, we conduct virtual consultations and work with local partners as needed.

You can schedule a consultation by filling out our contact form, calling our studio directly, or emailing us at info@akasstudio.com. We offer both in-person and virtual consultation options. Initial 30-minute consultations are complimentary, with more in-depth sessions available at our standard hourly rate.
