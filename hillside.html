Hillside Retreat Villa

A 5,000 sq ft luxury villa blending modern design with natural elements, nestled in the hills with panoramic views.

Project Overview

A contemporary hillside retreat designed to harmonize with its natural surroundings while offering luxurious modern living.

Property Type

Luxury Residential Villa

Size

5,000 sq ft (465 sq m)

Location

Malibu, California

Design Concept

The Hillside Retreat Villa was conceived as a seamless integration of architecture and landscape. The design emphasizes clean lines, expansive glass walls, and natural materials to create a dialogue between the built environment and the surrounding hills.

Key features include cantilevered decks, a floating staircase, and a green roof that blends the structure into the hillside. The interior palette combines warm woods, polished concrete, and steel accents for a sophisticated yet organic aesthetic.

Project Details

Challenges & Solutions

The steep hillside location presented significant engineering challenges. Our solution was to use a combination of deep concrete piers and a steel frame structure that cantilevers over the hillside, minimizing site disturbance while maximizing views.

Sustainable features include passive solar design, a rainwater collection system, and native drought-resistant landscaping that requires minimal irrigation.
