Urban Oasis Apartment

A contemporary apartment design that maximizes space in a 750 sq ft footprint.

Residential Modern Space-Saving

Project Overview

The Urban Oasis Apartment is a 750 square foot space in downtown Seattle that was transformed from a dark, cramped unit into a bright, functional modern home.

Our challenge was to create distinct living zones while maintaining an open feel, incorporate ample storage without sacrificing style, and bring natural elements into an urban environment.

The design features custom built-ins, multi-functional furniture, and a neutral palette with warm wood accents. Large mirrors and strategic lighting enhance the sense of space.
