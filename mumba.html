DW MUMBA

A signature of natural textures and honest materials an approach clearly expressed in this bespoke kitchen design. Rooted in a philosophy of refined simplicity, the space is intended to feel sophisticated, organic, and effortlessly contemporary. Every detail was selected with purpose, ensuring a tactile and visually rich experience that remains understated and timeless.

The kitchen is framed by a raw brick finish, lending a grounded, architectural presence that pays homage to traditional craftsmanship while introducing a layer of texture and warmth. This rawness is balanced by the clean lines of custom cabinetry and minimalist detailing, creating a harmonious interplay between rough and refined

Ultimately, this kitchen is a clear expression of our studio’s design ethos: modern yet rooted, tactile yet polished, and deeply attuned to the spirit of its context.
