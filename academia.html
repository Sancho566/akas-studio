IR ACADEMIA

This contemporary residential project by AKAS STUDIO was shaped by a rich interplay of materials, each selected to inform and inspire the creative journey. At the heart of the design process was the homeowner’s expansive and diverse art collection, which became a guiding force in shaping the spatial narrative.

Rather than competing with the artworks, the architecture and interiors were designed to support and enhance them creating a dynamic dialogue between art, materiality, and form. We embraced a strictly minimalist approach, allowing the pieces to breathe within the space while emphasizing clean lines, refined finishes, and intentional negative space.

The material palette is layered yet restrained: warm timber, soft textiles, and subtle natural stone. These surfaces act as quiet backdrops, amplifying the texture and colour of each artwork without overwhelming the environment. Every furnishing and fixture were chosen with clarity and restraint, contributing to a curated, gallery-like feel throughout the home.

Ultimately, this project reflects AKAS STUDIO’s commitment to highly personal, context-aware design. It’s a space where contemporary living meets artistic expression serene, sophisticated, and grounded in a deep respect for both art and architecture.
