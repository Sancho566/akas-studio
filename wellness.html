JV HOME WELLNESS CENTRE

we approach wellness spaces with the same design rigour and sensitivity as any other living area balancing aesthetics, function, and emotional wellbeing. This home wellness room and gym was envisioned as a holistic sanctuary that seamlessly blends performance and comfort, offering a versatile environment for strength training, cardio, and restorative practices.

Spatial planning was key: generous open areas allow for fluid movement between activities, while durable, high performance flooring supports intense workouts. Natural light plays a central role in energising the space by day, complemented by adjustable lighting to create the right mood at any hour. Full-length mirrors enhance spatial awareness, while discreet storage keeps equipment organized and the space visually calm
