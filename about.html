AKAS STUDIO is a distinguished interior architecture and design studio based in Capetown, South Africa, known for its modern, high-end approach and commitment to authentic luxury.

AKAS STUDIO is a premium interior architecture design studio founded by visionary designer Arthur Kamhoti, who trained at the prestigious BHC School of Design. His style blends authenticity and calm energy to create spaces that enrich life. We approach luxury as a timeless journey—merging storytelling, art, and science to craft serene spaces that prioritize the well-being of our discerning clients. Our studio specializes in bespoke interiors that redefine luxury through immersive experiences and refined detail, shaped by Arthur's passion and professional foundation. At AKAS STUDIO, we turn vision into timeless elegance—blending creativity with precision from concept to completion as your trusted design partner.
