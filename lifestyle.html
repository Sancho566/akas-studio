SNR LIFESTYLE

AKAS STUDIO, we had the pleasure of collaborating with our client, SNR, to craft a luxurious night lounge that elevates the guest experience through refined design and sensory immersion. From the outset, our shared vision was to create an exclusive environment that feels both indulgent and inviting, rooted in sophistication and timeless appeal. The space is meticulously curated with high-end finishes such as deep-toned marble, rich velvet upholstery, brushed brass accents, and dark-stained timber, all selected to withstand the demands of a high-traffic hospitality setting while maintaining an unmistakable sense of luxury. Thoughtful lighting design plays a pivotal role, with warm ambient glows, feature lighting, and backlit textures that shift the atmosphere seamlessly from evening to late night. Working closely with SNR, we ensured the layout fostered intimacy without compromising flow. Custom furnishings and layered materiality bring depth and personality to the space, while acoustics and spatial zoning ensure comfort at every touchpoint. This night lounge is not just a destination, it’s an experience. A perfect blend of opulence and authenticity, aligned with both SNR’s brand and AKAS STUDIO’s design philosophy of creating spaces that are bold, considered, and emotionally resonant.
